
Challenge
Write a program to generate a movie ticket displaying the person's name and cost of admission.  
The price of the movie will be $25 full price or $5.30 if the person was born before 1950 or after 2000.  
The program should ask the user to enter their name and birth year before printing out the tickets that would look like this:

Enter Your Name: 
Joe Bloggs
Please Enter your year of birth:
1970
|**********************|
|   -- ADMIT ONE --    |
|Name:       Josh Hortt|
|Price: $         25.00|
|----------------------|
Try this for yourself before looking at the attached solution.
